Smart energy monitor

Firmware, Hardware, Software

Worked with a team to develop a smart energy monitor. By reading the voltage across and current through a load, using a shunt resisitor, we were able to find the root mean square and peak values. We were then able to write these values to a 4 bit 7 segement display, UART transmission to a computer and and python application.
