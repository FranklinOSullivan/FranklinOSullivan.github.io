Cover letter

I am a third-year Computer Systems Engineering student at the University of Auckland.

This degree couples electronics and software engineering to teach an array of theoretical and practical skills related largely to computer architecture and embedded systems. Most noably this includes the teaching of theoretical software development including object orientated programming as well as use of many different data structures and algorithms to solve given problems quickly and effeciently. There is also emphasis on electronics design. Specifically, circuit design, simulation, breadboard testing, PCB development and soldering. Due to this, there has been a focus on considerations such as power used/ wasted, tricks like access to instantaneous current from localised capacitors and the reduction of noise throughout PCB layers.

Learning all of this has allowed me to pursure computer systems design for fun in my own time. Leading to the development of many of my own projects accross all three diciplines of electronics, computer systems and software.

I wish to work in a position where I am able to further develop my understanding of these fields as well as helping to improve my skills. I would love to work in firmware or embedded systems development. This is the part of the degree that I find the most enjoyable, and have the most experience in. The responsibility of working on cyber-physical systems requiring real-time, safety-critical systems would also be a huge bonus as it would teach me many industry skills that are not easily learned in university or through research.
